###  防火墙

```sh
service firewalld stop
systemctl enable firewalld

firewall-cmd --query-port=9300/tcp  # 查端口是否开放
firewall-cmd --add-port=9300/tcp --permanent # 防火墙永久性允许指定端口
firewall-cmd --reload
```


### ulimit
